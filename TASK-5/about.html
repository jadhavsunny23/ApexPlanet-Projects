About SRJ-Shop

SRJ-Shop is an e-commerce front-end project, which provides vast areas of products.

Features

  • Multipage front-end (Home, Products, Cart, About, Contact)
  • Search, category filter and sorting
  • Add/remove items to cart and simulated checkout
  • LocalStorage persistence of cart
